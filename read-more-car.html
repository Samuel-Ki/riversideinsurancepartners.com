Vehicle Coverage Options

Auto Insurance

Auto insurance can help protect you financially after an accident, vehicle damage, theft, or another covered event. Coverage can be selected based on your vehicle, driving habits, budget, and level of risk.

Available coverage may include:
  • Liability coverage
  • Collision coverage
  • Comprehensive coverage
  • Uninsured and underinsured motorist coverage
  • Medical payments coverage
  • Roadside assistance
  • Rental vehicle reimbursement
Ask About Auto Insurance

Motorcycle Insurance

Motorcycle insurance provides coverage designed for the risks associated with riding and owning a motorcycle. Policies may cover the motorcycle, the rider, passengers, accessories, and liability to others.

Available coverage may include:
  • Bodily injury and property damage liability
  • Collision coverage
  • Comprehensive coverage
  • Uninsured and underinsured motorist coverage
  • Passenger liability coverage
  • Custom parts and equipment coverage
  • Roadside assistance
Ask About Motorcycle Insurance

RV Insurance

Recreational vehicle insurance can help protect your motorhome, camper, or travel trailer. Coverage may apply while the RV is being driven, stored, or used as a temporary residence.

Available coverage may include:
  • Liability coverage
  • Collision and comprehensive coverage
  • Personal belongings coverage
  • Emergency expense coverage
  • Vacation liability coverage
  • Roadside assistance
  • Total loss replacement options
Ask About RV Insurance

Boat Insurance

Boat insurance can provide financial protection for your watercraft, equipment, passengers, and liability to others. Coverage can be tailored to the type of boat and how it is used.

Available coverage may include:
  • Physical damage coverage
  • Liability coverage
  • Medical payments coverage
  • Uninsured watercraft coverage
  • Personal property and equipment coverage
  • Emergency towing and assistance
  • Trailer coverage
